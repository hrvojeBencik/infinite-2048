```markdown
# Screenshot Capture Guide

## iOS — App Store Connect

### Required Sizes
| Label | Dimensions (Portrait) | Device/Simulator |
|-------|----------------------|-----------------|
| 6.9" display | 1260 x 2736 px | iPhone 16 Pro Max simulator |
| 6.5" display | 1284 x 2778 px OR 1242 x 2688 px | iPhone 15 Plus or iPhone 11 Pro Max simulator |
| iPad 13" | 2064 x 2752 px | iPad Pro 13-inch (M4) simulator |

Note: 6.7" does NOT have its own slot — covered by 6.9" set.

### Required Scenes (minimum 3 per size)
1. **Active Gameplay** — 4x4 board with high-value tiles (512+), non-zero score (4000+), all power-ups visible and enabled, Genesis Zone (purple accent)
2. **Level Select / Zone Overview** — All 5 zones visible with accent colors, at least 2 zones showing partial completion with stars
3. **Level Complete Dialog** — Confetti burst active, 3 stars displayed, score and XP shown, Share button visible

### Capture Steps
1. Run app on each simulator size: `flutter run -d "iPhone 16 Pro Max"`
2. Navigate to the appropriate screen
3. Set up the game state (play a few levels to get high-value tiles and zone progress)
4. Take screenshot: Cmd+S in iOS Simulator (saves to Desktop)
5. Repeat for each scene and each device size
6. Upload raw screenshots (no device frames, no text overlays) to App Store Connect

## Android — Google Play Console

### Required Sizes
| Device | Minimum | Recommended |
|--------|---------|-------------|
| Phone | 1080 x 1920 px | 1080 x 1920 px |
| 7" Tablet | 1200 x 1920 px | 1600 x 2560 px |
| 10" Tablet | 1600 x 2560 px | 2048 x 2732 px |

### Same 3 scenes as iOS
Capture on Android emulator or physical device. Minimum 2 screenshots for phone; 1 for each tablet size.

### Status Bar
- Hide status bar or ensure it shows clean state (no carrier text, battery full)
- iOS convention: time shows 9:41
```

```markdown
# Google Play Data Safety Form — Field-by-Field Guide

## Overview
This guide tells you exactly what to select in Google Play Console -> App Content -> Data Safety.

## Does your app collect or share any of the required user data types?
**Answer: Yes**

## Data Types to Declare

### 1. Device or other IDs
- **Collected:** Yes
- **Shared:** Yes (shared with Google AdMob for advertising purposes)
- **Processing purpose:** Advertising or marketing
- **Is this data required or can users choose whether to share it?** Data collection is required (cannot use app without it)
- **Encrypted in transit:** Yes
- **Can users request deletion:** No (managed by Google AdMob SDK)

### 2. App interactions
- **Collected:** Yes
- **Shared:** Yes (shared with Firebase Analytics)
- **Processing purpose:** Analytics
- **Is this data required?** Data collection is required
- **Encrypted in transit:** Yes
- **Can users request deletion:** No

### 3. App info and performance (Crash logs)
- **Collected:** Yes
- **Shared:** Yes (shared with Firebase Crashlytics)
- **Processing purpose:** App functionality, Analytics
- **Is this data required?** Data collection is required
- **Encrypted in transit:** Yes
- **Can users request deletion:** No

### 4. Personal info (Name, Email) — CONDITIONAL
- **Collected:** Only if user signs in via Firebase Auth (Google/Apple Sign-In)
- **If sign-in is optional:** Mark as "Users can choose whether this data is collected"
- **Shared:** No
- **Processing purpose:** Account management
- **Encrypted in transit:** Yes
- **Can users request deletion:** Yes (Firebase Auth supports account deletion)

## Questions to answer "No"
- Does your app collect Financial info? **No**
- Does your app collect Location? **No**
- Does your app collect Health and fitness? **No**
- Does your app collect Messages? **No**
- Does your app collect Photos and videos? **No**
- Does your app collect Audio? **No**
- Does your app collect Files and docs? **No**
- Does your app collect Calendar? **No**
- Does your app collect Contacts? **No**

## Privacy Policy
- **Privacy policy URL:** https://hrvojebencik.github.io/infinite-2048/privacy-policy.html

## References
- AdMob disclosure guide: https://developers.google.com/admob/android/privacy/play-data-disclosure
```
