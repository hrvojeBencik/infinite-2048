Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 16, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how 2048: Merge Quest ("we", "us", or "the App") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Account Information: If you choose to sign in using Google or Apple Sign-In, we receive your name, email address, and profile picture as provided by the authentication provider.
  • Game Data: Your game progress, scores, achievements, and statistics are stored locally on your device and optionally synced to our cloud services.

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Analytics Data: We use Firebase Analytics to collect anonymized usage data such as app opens, screens viewed, game events, and general engagement metrics. This helps us improve the game experience.
  • Device Information: We may collect device type, operating system version, and app version for compatibility and debugging purposes.
  • Advertising Data: We use Google AdMob to display ads. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data to serve relevant advertisements. Please refer to Google's Privacy Policy for more details.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the game experience
  • To save and sync your game progress across devices (if signed in)
  • To display leaderboards and compare scores
  • To serve advertisements through Google AdMob
  • To analyze usage patterns and improve the app
  • To provide customer support

3. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services that may collect information:

  • Firebase (Google): Authentication, analytics, remote configuration, and cloud storage. Firebase Privacy Policy
  • Google AdMob: Advertisement serving. Google Privacy Policy
  • RevenueCat: In-app purchase management. RevenueCat Privacy Policy

4. Data Storage and Security

Your game data is primarily stored locally on your device using encrypted local storage. If you sign in with an account, certain data (profile, scores, achievements) may be synced to Firebase cloud services, which are protected by industry-standard security measures.

5. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us so we can promptly delete it.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Opt out of personalized advertising (through your device settings)
  • Play the game without signing in (limited features)

7. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the game services. If you delete your account, we will delete your personal data within 30 days, except where retention is required by law.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the new policy within the App. Your continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
